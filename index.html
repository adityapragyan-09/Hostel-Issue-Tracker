📖 How to use the Issue Tracker

  • 1️⃣ Navigate to Submit Complaint from the sidebar menu.
  • 2️⃣ Fill in all required fields — your name, room number, category, priority, and a detailed description of the issue.
  • 3️⃣ Optionally attach a photo of the issue to help the hostel team understand the problem better.
  • 4️⃣ Click Submit Complaint and you'll receive a unique Complaint ID for tracking.
  • 5️⃣ Go to My Complaints to view, search, and filter all your submitted complaints and their current status.
  • 6️⃣ If your issue is urgent, please also contact the hostel warden or emergency helpline directly.
